Interview introduction
Opening Good morning / afternoon. I'm Sajid Ahmad, an SAP Security and GRC Consultant with around 5 years of IT experience, including more than 3 years specializing in SAP Security. I'm currently working with Capgemini, where I joined in July 2021.
Expertise My core expertise covers SAP ECC, S/4HANA, Fiori Security, and SAP GRC Access Control — including ARA, ARM, EAM, and BRM. Day-to-day I handle user administration, role design, authorization troubleshooting, SU24 updates, transport management, and GRC workflow handling.
Current project At GE Vernova, I've been part of an S/4HANA implementation and cloud migration — designing security roles, Fiori catalogs, spaces, OData services, and authorization concepts. I also support SOX compliance and audit requirements.
Previous project At Herff Jones, I handled end-to-end SAP Security implementation — Fiori configuration, GRC access management, and SoD risk mitigation.
Strengths & goal My strengths lie in authorization troubleshooting using SU53, ST01, and STAUTHTRACE, and collaborating with cross-functional teams for secure, compliant SAP solutions. I'm looking for an opportunity to deepen my expertise in S/4HANA and GRC.
